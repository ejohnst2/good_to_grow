Good to Grow

Turn your excess space into an abundant food forest

Why?

There are between 40 to 50 million acres of yard in American households. As more people look to connect back with earth, it's a perfect opportunity to become a home grower. Converting spaces into edible plants contributes to the fight for food justice and increases the carbon sequestration from your land. The first step is knowing what's good to grow.

How it works

  1. Fill out Details

    Take 5 minutes to tell us about where you are, your growing objectives, and when you plan to start

  2. Let algorithms work

    Our algorithms do the heavy lifting by work with several geocoding, plant and climate APIs to generate your report.

  3. Receive personalized report

    Within minutes, you will have a personalized recommendation on what's good to grow!
